About Onnyx

We're on a mission to democratize stock trading by making it accessible, affordable, and easy for everyone.

Our Story

Onnyx was founded in 2024 with a simple vision: to revolutionize how people invest in stocks. We saw that traditional brokers were expensive, complex, and intimidating for new investors.

Our team of experienced traders and engineers came together to build a platform that combines cutting-edge technology with user-friendly design. We believe that everyone deserves access to the tools and knowledge needed to invest confidently.

Today, Onnyx serves thousands of traders worldwide, helping them achieve their financial goals without the burden of commission fees.
